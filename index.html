Streamline Client Invoicing and Internal Payment Splits

InvoiceHound simplifies the payment process for freelance teams, ensuring timely payments and reduced administrative hassle

Key Features

  • Automated Invoicing: Generate a single invoice for your client with consolidated earnings from all team members
  • Internal Payment Split Logic: Automatically split earnings among team members based on logged hours
  • Payment Reminder Escalation: Send polite reminders on Day 1 via Slack, firm reminders on Day 7 via Slack, and formal invoices with payment details on Day 14 via email
  • Hour Logging and Tracking: Allow team members to log hours worked on a project, with automatic calculation of earnings
  • Invoice History and Status: Provide a record of all invoices sent, with status updates on payment receipt and internal payment splits
