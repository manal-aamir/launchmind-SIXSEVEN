Streamline Your Invoicing and Payment Process

Get paid on time with InvoiceHound, the ultimate tool for freelance teams.

Features

  • Automated Invoicing: Generate a single invoice for your client based on hours logged by team members.
  • Internal Payment Split Logic: Split earnings among team members based on their logged hours.
  • Payment Reminder Escalation: Send reminders to clients on Day 1 (polite Slack), Day 7 (firm Slack), and Day 14 (formal email with HTML invoice).
  • Hour Tracking: Allow team members to log their hours worked on a project.
  • Invoice History: Maintain a record of all invoices sent to clients, including payment status and reminders sent.
