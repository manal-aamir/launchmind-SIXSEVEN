Streamlined Invoicing for Freelance Teams

Get paid on time with InvoiceHound

InvoiceHound simplifies the invoicing process for freelance teams, ensuring timely payments and reducing administrative burden.

Features

  • Automated Invoicing: Generate a single invoice for your client with a summary of work done by all team members
  • Internal Payment Split: Split earnings among team members based on logged hours
  • Payment Reminder Escalation: Send reminders to clients on Day 1 (polite Slack), Day 7 (firm Slack), and Day 14 (formal email with HTML invoice)
  • Payment Tracking: Track payment status and send notifications to team members when payment is received
  • Invoice History: Maintain a record of all invoices sent to clients, including payment status and reminders sent
