Streamline Your Invoicing and Payment Tracking

Get paid on time with InvoiceHound, the ultimate tool for freelance teams.

Features

  • Automated Invoicing: Generate a single invoice for your client with a summary of work completed
  • Internal Payment Split Logic: Split earnings among team members based on logged hours
  • Payment Reminder Escalation: Send reminders on Day 1 (polite Slack), Day 7 (firm Slack), and Day 14 (formal email with HTML invoice)
  • Payment Tracking and Notifications: Track payment status and notify team members when payment is received
  • Invoice History and Analytics: Maintain a record of all invoices and provide insights into payment trends and team performance
