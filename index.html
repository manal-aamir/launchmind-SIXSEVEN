Features

Unified Client Invoicing

Generate a single, professional invoice for clients, simplifying payment processes and reducing errors.

Hour-Based Internal Splits

Automatically calculate and split earnings among team members based on hours logged, ensuring fairness and transparency.

Escalating Payment Reminders

Send automated reminders to clients on Day 1, Day 7, and Day 14, escalating from polite Slack nudges to formal email notifications with embedded HTML invoices.

AI-Driven Reminder Emails

Utilize AI to craft reminder emails with controlled tone and language, minimizing awkwardness and maximizing payment response rates.

Payment Notification and Settlement

Notify team members of their earnings once the client pays, facilitating timely and accurate team settlements and reducing payment-related stress.

How It Works

  1. Log Hours: Track the hours worked by each team member on a project.
  2. Send Invoice: Generate a single, professional invoice for the client, simplifying payment processes and reducing errors.
  3. Get Paid + Auto-Reminders: Receive payment from the client and automatically send reminders to ensure timely payment.
