Freelance teams deserve payments without the awkward silence.

Simplify payment management with InvoiceHound.

Features

Unified Client Invoicing

Generate a single, professional invoice for clients, simplifying payment management and reducing errors.

Hour-Based Internal Payment Split

Automatically split earnings among team members based on hours worked, ensuring fair and accurate compensation.

Escalating Payment Reminders

Send automated reminders to clients on Day 1, Day 7, and Day 14, escalating from polite Slack nudges to formal email notifications with HTML invoices.

AI-Driven Reminder Emails

Utilize AI to craft reminder emails with tone control, ensuring that notifications are professional, yet firm, to prompt timely payments from clients.

Payment Notification and Team Settlement

Notify team members of their earnings once the client has paid, streamlining internal settlement and reducing administrative overhead.

How it works

  1. Log Hours: Track the hours worked by each team member.
  2. Send Invoice: Generate a single, professional invoice for the client.
  3. Get Paid + Auto-Reminders: Receive payment from the client and send automated reminders if necessary.
