Streamline Client Invoicing with InvoiceHound

Automate invoicing, internal payment splits, and reminder escalation for freelance teams

Features

  • Automated Invoicing: Generate a single invoice for your client, aggregating hours logged by all team members
  • Internal Payment Split Logic: Automatically split earnings among team members based on logged hours
  • Payment Reminder Escalation: Send reminders to clients on Day 1 (polite Slack), Day 7 (firm Slack), and Day 14 (formal email with HTML invoice)
  • Hour Tracking and Logging: Easily log hours worked on projects, enabling accurate invoicing and payment splits
  • Invoice History and Analytics: Track invoice status, payment history, and team performance metrics
